Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a safe, clean, and regularly updated landing page for delivering quick, snappy spurts of motivation wrapped in quotes, links to opensource content, and uplifting media designed to help people elevate their perspective of life. Whether its a quick quote to a statement, question, or an invoking question for others to pursue their aspirations, this is simply again a page to satisfy those attempts to reach people and lift them higher than they were before. None of the content would be advertized, self-serving, or with any particular agenda other than to leave people with hope, even if its merely from what words alone can deliver.

Audience

The intended audience would be targeted towards an open demographic that is not limited to anyone in specific, as everyone to a degree struggles with their own sense of empowerment from time to time, this would be intended towards aiding people in finding resources to lift, inspire, and help them better coach themselves on elevating their overall perspective of life.

Dynamic elements

Some elements that I could add to make my webpage engaging, useful and push me to further learn js would be to for example: Make an interactive menu (drop-down, hover, and click DOM event handlers), I could add a Carousel/Slider that dynamically makes images or even text change as I slide down the webpage (making both mobile and desktop users experience some level of engagement), could add a light/dark toggle in the header, and maybe even provide a Analytics Chart that displays how many people have signed up for a weekly newsletter, or have visited the webpage and unlocked the daily quote (triggered and opened based off of a button on the webpage?). These are just a few of the ideas that I have had while brainstorming this week.
